DID YOU KNOW?
“My Neighbor Totoro” is based on the childhood of Hayao Miyazaki. When Miyazaki and his siblings were young, their mother suffered a long illness and spent a lot of time in the hospital as the mother in the film. All that suffering was channeled by Miyazaki to build the story of "My Neighbor Totoro".
